# Product Overview

SafeText is a single-purpose web application that helps users identify suspicious SMS messages by analyzing them for common scam patterns. It provides instant risk assessment, detects specific red flags, and offers clear recommendations on appropriate actions.

## Key Characteristics

- **Client-side only**: No backend dependencies, works completely offline
- **Mobile-first**: Optimized for users checking messages on their phones
- **Single file**: Distributed as one self-contained HTML file
- **Instant analysis**: Pattern-based detection with results in under 1 second
- **Educational**: Explains what makes messages suspicious and what scammers want

## Target Users

Users who receive suspicious SMS messages and need quick guidance on whether they're legitimate or scams. Designed to be shareable with family members who may be vulnerable to scams.
